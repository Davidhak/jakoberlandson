Jakob Erlandson is a music producer and sound engineer based in Lund and Malmö, Sweden, working with music, film and live sound.

ABOUT ME

After graduating from the audio engineering programme at Luleå University of Technology, I've been working with music, film and live sound. Doing this, I've had the blessing of meeting several creative and inspiring people that, like me, always strive for creating something as beautiful and engaging as possible.

Naturally, I want to continue working on new projects, meet new people and continue to learn and grow from it. So, if you need any help with your creative project or just want to have a talk, let me know and I will contribute in any way I can!
